TIPS TO GET STARTED

Dribbling
players use their fingertip to push ball downwards, keeping their body in a ready position and their head up
Passing
involves accurately throwing the ball to a teammate to maintain possession and move it up the court
Shooting
The action of throwing the ball towards the goal to score points, which can be done from various position
Blocking
Using the body to block opponents from moving towards the goal
Positioning
Taking small, fast steps to avoid over-committing, angling the body to direct the opponent away from the goal area
Grip/Handling
Constantly handling the ball helps players become comfortable with its feel and how to transition it for delivery
